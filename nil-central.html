Quick Tips

The most important things every high school athlete needs to know before entering any NIL deal.

📋

Always Disclose First

Disclose all NIL deals to your school's athletic director before signing anything. Failure to disclose can jeopardize your eligibility.

🚫

No School Marks

Never use your school's logo, uniform, colors, or mascot in any NIL deal without explicit written permission from the principal and AD.

💰

It's Taxable Income

All NIL compensation is taxable. Once you start earning, consult a tax professional. Parents should be involved in this process.

📝

Get It In Writing

Verbal agreements are not enforceable. Every NIL deal needs a written contract. If you're a minor, a parent or guardian must co-sign.

⚠️

No Pay-for-Play

NIL compensation must be for your name, image, and likeness only — never tied to your athletic performance, stats, or wins.

🏷️

Label Sponsored Content

Any paid social media post must be labeled as a paid partnership using #ad or #sponsored. This is required by the FTC for all ages.

📱

Your Social = Your Brand

Brands look at your social media before reaching out. Keep public accounts professional, clean, and football-focused.

⭐

Know Your Value First

Use the NIL Rating Tool above to understand your market value before approaching any brand or signing any deal.
